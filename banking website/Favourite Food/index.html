Spaghetti and fried chicken stew

Ingredients:

    For the fried chicken:

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Vegetable oil for frying

    • For the spaghetti stew:

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Fry the chicken: In a shallow dish, mix together flour, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Pour buttermilk into a separate shallow dish. Dip chicken pieces into buttermilk, then coat in flour mixture. Fry in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through. Drain on paper towels.
  2. Cook spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  3. Make stew: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute. Stir in crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, dried basil, and dried oregano. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Combine spaghetti and stew: Add cooked spaghetti to the stew pot, tossing to combine. If needed, add some reserved pasta water to achieve desired consistency.
  5. Add fried chicken: Add fried chicken pieces to the spaghetti stew and toss gently to combine.
  6. Serve and enjoy: Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or basil, if desired.
